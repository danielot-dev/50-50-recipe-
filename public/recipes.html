Nigerian Cooking Tips from Our Experts

Perfect Jollof Rice

For authentic Jollof rice, use parboiled rice and cook on medium heat to prevent burning. The bottom layer (bottom pot) is considered a delicacy!

Egusi Soup Secrets

Toast your ground egusi (melon seeds) before adding to soup for enhanced flavor. Always use fresh spinach and add it last to maintain its vibrant color.

Pounded Yam Technique

For smooth pounded yam, ensure yams are fully cooked and use a mortar and pestle or food processor. Add warm water gradually for perfect consistency.
